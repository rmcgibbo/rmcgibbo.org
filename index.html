Robert T. McGibbon

Robert T. McGibbon

I am currently employed as a researcher at Hudson River Trading, where I develop machine learning and artificial intelligence algorithms to improve automated trading strategies.

From 2016 to 2025, I worked in the force field group at D.E. Shaw Research. While there, I built machine learning and physics-based potentials for molecular dynamics simulations of prospective drugs and the biological targets they interact with.

I earned my Ph.D. in Chemistry at Stanford University (2016), where I worked with Vijay Pande on long-timescale molecular dynamics simulations and time series analysis. My thesis is available here. I earned my bachelor's degree at Princeton University (2011), where I studied Chemistry and Computer Science and worked with Andrew Bocarsly.
